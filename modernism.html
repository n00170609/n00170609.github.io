MODERNISM & HYGIENE
EXHIBITION CATALOGUE

Concept:

The brief was to take one of our own written submissions from our Critical Contextual Studies and use it as a starting point for an exhibition and accompanying catalogue. Due to the coronavirus pandemic we could not scout out locations and see physical spaces. I was assigned SFMOMA as my exhibition location as there is extenisve information and pictures online. Further emphasis was therefore put on the catalogue and the concept for the exhibition.

Objectives and Audience:

My exhibiton was based on my term one essay ‘A Clean Utopia: Modernism and Hygiene’. I wanted the visitor/reader to understand the historical context behind modernism and how the same values can be seen and re-interpreted in a contemporary context.
