CALYPSO TYPE SPECIMEN
PRINT & WEB DESIGN

Concept:

The brief was split in two parts; firstly to design and code a microsite and secondly to design and print a booklet/pamphlet. They were both to promote the release of a new typeface across different media. The micro-site was completed first and used as a starting point for the print solution. I was assigned the typeface ‘Calypso’ by Typolar type foundry. It has thick slab-serifs and a square form which give it a dominating presence. I wanted to emphasise its use as a display face and give it a bold and brash personality.

Objectives and Audience:

The target audience for this project was designers and typographers. It was therefore important that the type specimen reflected the primary use of the typeface and its personality.
Booklet Cover

Calypso is a display face that has a very dominating presence due to its blocky form. The tone of voice was therefore bold
and unapologetic.
